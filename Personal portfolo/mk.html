BMW is a German multinational company which produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles. The company was founded in 1916 and has its headquarters in Munich, Germany. BMW is known for its high-performance cars and innovative technology.

BMW offers a wide range of vehicles, including sedans, SUVs, and sports cars. Some of the popular models include the BMW 3 Series, BMW 5 Series, and BMW X5. The company also produces electric vehicles under the BMW i sub-brand.

BMW is committed to sustainability and has set ambitious goals to reduce its carbon footprint and promote electric mobility. The company has invested heavily in research and development to create more efficient and environmentally friendly vehicles.
